About the role:

Are you ready to set the vision and strategy for building scalable, secure, and resilient platforms that power global LSEG Risk Intelligence products? We’re looking for a Principal/Senior Lead DevOps Engineer to define release architecture, influence technology direction, and lead transformational initiatives across our cloud-native infrastructure.

What You’ll Drive & Deliver

As a Principal DevOps Engineer, you’ll be a strategic technical leader, shaping the future of platform engineering and DevOps practices at scale:

Own the release & deployment architecture and roadmap for cloud-native infrastructure, ensuring scalability, security, and resilience.
Design and implement enterprise-grade automation frameworks using Terraform, CloudFormation, and GitLab workflows.
Set standards for CI/CD pipelines across multiple teams, driving best practices for secure and rapid delivery.
Lead Kubernetes platform strategy (Amazon EKS, Istio Service Mesh) for service-oriented architectures and multi-region deployments.
Champion Site Reliability Engineering principles, defining observability, reliability, and incident response strategies.
Influence technology adoption, evaluating emerging tools and practices to enhance developer experience and operational excellence.
Mentor senior engineers and technical leads, fostering a culture of innovation, automation, and continuous improvement.
Collaborate with senior leadership to align infrastructure strategy with business objectives and product roadmaps.

️ Your Tech Arsenal

We’re looking for someone with deep technical expertise and strategic vision in:

Cloud platforms: AWS (EC2, Lambda, RDS, S3, VPC, CloudFront, IAM, CloudWatch, etc.)
Infrastructure as Code: Terraform, CloudFormation
Container orchestration: Kubernetes (EKS), Istio Service Mesh
CI/CD tooling: GitLab CI, Jenkins, Bitbucket
Scripting & automation: Bash/Shell, Python
Security & compliance: IAM, KMS, encryption, DevSecOps practices
Monitoring & observability: Datadog, Prometheus, Grafana, ELK, or similar
Agile & DevOps culture: GitOps, SRE principles, Agile methodologies

✅ What You Bring

8+ years in DevOps or platform engineering, with 3+ years in senior leadership or principal-level roles.
Proven experience architecting and operating cloud-native platforms at enterprise scale.
Ability to influence technology strategy and drive adoption across multiple teams.
Exceptional communication and stakeholder management skills.
A passion for innovation, automation, and developer enablement.
Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or equivalent experience.

Leadership Impact

As a Senior Lead DevOps Engineer, you will:

Shape the technical vision for cloud-native infrastructure across the organization.
Influence senior stakeholders and executives, ensuring DevOps strategy aligns with business priorities.
Drive cultural transformation, embedding DevOps and SRE principles across engineering teams.
Act as a thought leader, representing the organization in internal forums and external communities.
Enable scale, ensuring platforms support global growth and innovation.
