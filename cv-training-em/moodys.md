Skills And Competencies

Extensive experience (8+ years) building and shipping production-grade software systems or services, including distributed systems and large-scale data processing; 10+ years and Engineering Manager/Technical Lead experience preferred
Proven ability to design, build, and operate online services and fault-tolerant distributed systems at internet scale, including cloud service development (Azure or AWS)
Demonstrable proficiency in C++/C#/Java, REST APIs, agile and test-driven development, and secure software design concepts
Hands-on experience with artificial intelligence concepts and tools to streamline engineering workflows and address complex business challenges; awareness of AI risk management and ethical use
Strong problem-solving, debugging, and data-driven iterative improvement skills; familiarity with microservices, DevSecOps, and cloud-scale distributed design patterns
Experience building and scaling full-stack engineering teams across geographies, architecting and delivering distributed cloud services, and working with modern infrastructure tools (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform, GitHub Actions, Jenkins, Azure DevOps, Datadog, Prometheus, Grafana, ELK stack) preferred

Education

BS/MS/PhD in Computer Science or equivalent industry experience
