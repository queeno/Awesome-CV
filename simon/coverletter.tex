  \lettersection{About Me}
  I am a hands-on Engineering Leader and Cloud-Native Architect with over 15 years of experience

  \lettersection{Why You?}
  I am drawn to organisations that view platform engineering not just as infrastructure, but as a strategic
  multiplier for product velocity. Your commitment to innovation and solving hard technical
  problems aligns perfectly with my own philosophy. I am particularly excited by the opportunity
  to apply my experience in designing "Golden Path" platforms and event-driven architectures to
  help your teams move faster, safely. Whether it is modernising legacy estates or building
  greenfield AI-ready infrastructure, I want to be part of a culture that obsessively focuses on
  Developer Experience (DevEx) and operational excellence.

  \lettersection{Why Me?}
  Throughout my career, I have consistently bridged the gap between business strategy and technical
  execution. At JPMC, I scaled a Cloud Enablement unit from 3 to 50+ engineers, architecting a
  firm-wide GCP foundation. At Barclays, I acted as the Technical Authority for a payments incubator,
  modernising critical rails on AWS. I bring deep expertise in Cloud-Native ecosystems (Kubernetes,
  Terraform, Crossplane) and a proven track record of embedding security and compliance (NIST, ISO)
  without sacrificing speed. I don't just manage teams; I code alongside them, fostering a culture of
  ownership, curiosity, and high trust. I am ready to bring this blend of strategic vision and hands-on
  capability to drive your engineering goals forward.
